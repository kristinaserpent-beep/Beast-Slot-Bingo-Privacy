Privacy Policy for Beast Slot Bingo

Last updated: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Beast Slot Bingo ("the Game", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile game.

1. Information We Collect

Beast Slot Bingo does not require you to create an account in order to play. We do not intentionally collect personal information such as your full name, home address, or personal identification number.

However, the Game may process limited information necessary for functionality, purchases, performance, security, and compliance. This may include:

  • Device information such as device model, operating system, and app version
  • Gameplay-related data stored locally on your device, such as game progress, settings, and coin balance
  • Purchase-related information needed to process in-app purchases through the app store provider
  • Basic diagnostics, crash, and performance information if enabled through platform or third-party services

2. In-App Purchases

Beast Slot Bingo offers optional in-app purchases, including the purchase of virtual coins. All payments are processed by the platform provider, such as Google Play, and not directly by us.

We do not receive or store your full payment card details. We may receive limited purchase-related information, such as confirmation that a purchase was completed, in order to deliver the purchased virtual items inside the Game.

3. How We Use Information

Any information processed in connection with the Game is used only for legitimate purposes such as:

  • Providing and operating the Game
  • Saving your local progress and settings
  • Delivering purchased virtual coins or other in-game content
  • Maintaining app stability, security, and performance
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Data Storage

Certain game data, such as your progress, settings, and virtual coin balance, may be stored locally on your device so that the Game can function properly.

If third-party platform services are used, some limited technical or transaction-related information may also be processed by those providers according to their own privacy policies.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We may rely on trusted third-party services, such as app store providers or technical service providers, where necessary to operate the Game, process purchases, or maintain performance and security. Those providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

6. Third-Party Services

The Game may use third-party services made available through the mobile platform or integrated tools. These services may process limited data necessary for app distribution, purchase handling, crash reporting, analytics, fraud prevention, or technical delivery.

Examples may include:

  • Google Play / Google Play Billing
  • Unity services or other game engine related services, if enabled
  • Crash reporting or analytics tools, if enabled

Please review the privacy policies of those third-party providers for more information about how they handle data.

7. Children’s Privacy

Beast Slot Bingo is not directed to young children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the Game, please contact us so we can review the situation.

8. Data Retention

Because much of the Game’s data may be stored locally on your device, retention can depend on how long you keep the Game installed and whether you delete app data or uninstall the Game.

Transaction-related records may be retained by platform providers as required for legal, tax, accounting, security, and fraud-prevention purposes.

9. Your Rights
